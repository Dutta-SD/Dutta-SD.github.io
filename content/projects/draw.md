Radiotherapy planning requires oncologists to manually delineate anatomical structures in CT scans — a process that can take hours per patient and varies significantly between clinicians. DRAW is an end-to-end autosegmentation workflow that automates this using deep learning, built entirely on open-source software so it can be adopted without expensive commercial licensing.

I contributed to the system engineering and the multi-hospital rollout during my time at IIT Kharagpur's VIP Lab. The workflow was validated across multiple hospitals and is in preliminary clinical use at **Tata Medical Center, Kolkata**. The paper covers the full system design, integration decisions, and preliminary clinical results.

Published in *The Royal College of Radiologists Open*, 2025.

**Paper:** [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.rcro.2025.100210)
